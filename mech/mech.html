Custom Nerf gun built to gain experience with Onshape. Based on a Rival Perses, the design is 40% smaller, has 80% of the origional capacity, and shoots 30% faster.

[WIP] Custom model rockets. The larger of the two is designed to fit an arduino nano, which will serve as a custom flight computer that reads from a barometer and checks the result with an accelerometer to handle parachute deployment. That setup will allow me to run custom rocket motors that lack the standard ejection charge to reduce production complexity. A side effect is the arduino can also log height reached.
